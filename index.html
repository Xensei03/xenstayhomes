Common Questions

Everything you want to know

Is 10% really all-inclusive? +

Yes. Our 10% management fee covers listing creation, guest communication, turnover coordination, dynamic pricing, and monthly reporting. There are no setup fees, no maintenance markups, and no hidden charges. The only extras are direct cleaning costs, which are passed through at cost to the owner.

How does the $1,000 lease signing bonus work? +

For long-term rental properties, we pay you a $1,000 bonus every time we successfully secure a new tenant and execute a signed lease. This is our way of rewarding owners who grow with us.

What's the difference between your STR and LTR fees? +

Short-term rentals (Airbnb/VRBO) are managed at 10% of monthly gross bookings. Long-term rentals are managed at 6% of monthly gross rent — plus the $1,000 lease signing bonus each time a new tenant is placed.

How do owner payouts work? +

We pay owners monthly with a detailed statement showing gross revenue, the management fee, and your net payout. Payments are made directly to your bank account. You'll always know exactly what you earned and what we charged.

Do you operate outside the Sacramento area? +

Yes — we currently manage properties across Sacramento, Roseville, Auburn, Orangevale, Folsom, Rancho Cordova, Granite Bay, and Yuba City. We're actively expanding statewide across California. If your property is in CA, reach out and we'll let you know if we can service your market.

How long does it take to get my property listed? +

After signing our management agreement, most properties are live and accepting bookings within 5–7 business days. This includes photo coordination, listing creation, pricing setup, and platform optimization.

What happens if a guest damages the property? +

We coordinate all damage claims through Airbnb's AirCover program, which provides up to $3M in host protection. We handle the documentation and claims process on your behalf so you don't have to deal with the back-and-forth.

Is there a long-term contract? +

Our standard management agreements are straightforward with no unreasonable lock-in periods. We believe our results speak for themselves — owners stay because they're happy, not because they're trapped.
